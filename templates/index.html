About Us

Development Project Planning Solution (DPPS)

Development Project Planning Solution Limited is a company based in Zanzibar, that specializes in Project Development and all project-related activities, including mapping and architectural drawings.

Additionally, the company offers short-term training in various fields such as Management and Leadership, Customer Service, Project Proposal Writing, and Grant Project Proposal Writing. The company also handles all aspects of project development, including writing Business Plans, Strategic Plans, and Action Plans.
