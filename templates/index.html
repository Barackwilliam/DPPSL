About Us

Development Project Planning Solution (DPPS)

Development Project Planning Solution Limited is a Zanzibar-based company specializing in Project Development and all project-related activities, including mapping and architectural drawings.

Additionally, we offer short-term training in fields such as Management and Leadership, Customer Service, Project Proposal Writing, and Grant Project Proposal Writing. We also handle all aspects of project development, including Business Plans, Strategic Plans, and Action Plans.
